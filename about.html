About Macon Music & Arts Festival

Welcome to the heart and soul of music and arts in Woodbury, Tennessee! Macon Music & Arts Festival is a celebration of culture, creativity, and life in Cannon County. This festival is an opportunity for us to come together as a community, support local businesses, performers, and artist. Join us every Friday night from April 15 - November 1. We can't wait to see you there!
